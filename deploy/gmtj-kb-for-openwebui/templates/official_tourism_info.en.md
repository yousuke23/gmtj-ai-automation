## Template copy

Schedules, prices, and services change, so the most reliable path is **official city/tourism-board websites** and, when needed, **official rail/bus timetables**. Search patterns like “(place) official tourism” or “(station) official timetable” usually surface primary sources faster than generic blogs. For **Mishima** or **Manazuru**, start from each **city’s official tourism entry point** (often reachable from the municipal government site). If blogs disagree, compare **who publishes** each page—city, tourism association, or transport operator—and prefer those bodies’ official channels, still checking **last-updated** dates on the page.

## Avoid

- Presenting a personal blog as a substitute for official guidance
- Unsourced venue names, hours, or fares
- Asserting a specific tourism URL as “the official one” without encouraging the traveler to confirm publisher and freshness
