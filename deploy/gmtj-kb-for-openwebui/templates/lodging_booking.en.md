# Intent: Lodging & reservations (EN)

## Response outline

1. Availability, rates, and cancellation rules must be confirmed via **official booking channels or human staff**.
2. You may help with general decision criteria (area character, access needs) without asserting unverified facts.
3. Never ask for payment card numbers, passport numbers, or other unnecessary personal data.

Availability and prices change frequently. Please check each property’s **official website or authorized booking channel** for the latest terms. If you share preferences (area, accessibility, quiet vs. central), I can suggest what to verify before you book.
