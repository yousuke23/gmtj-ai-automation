Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

This Policy describes our privacy practices for the Services.

1. Introduction

Japan Music Tourism ("JMT", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our websites and related online services, including any AI-assisted dialogue features (the "Services").

2. Information we collect

We may collect: information you submit via forms, email, or chat; technical data such as IP address, device type, browser, language, referring URLs, and timestamps; cookies and similar technologies; analytics events; and, when you use online dialogue features, the content of your messages and model responses. The specific items collected may change as we update the Services; we will reflect material changes in this Policy.

3. How we use information

We use information to operate, maintain, and improve the Services; respond to inquiries; secure our systems; comply with law; analyze usage in aggregate or de-identified form; develop new features; and evaluate AI safety and quality where applicable. We do not use your personal information for automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects solely by automated means, unless we provide a separate lawful basis and explanation.

4. Legal bases

Where GDPR or UK GDPR applies, we rely on contract, legitimate interests, consent, or legal obligation as appropriate for each processing activity. You may request further information about legal bases by contacting us.

5. Sharing and subprocessors

We use trusted vendors for hosting, analytics, email delivery, security monitoring, and—where applicable—model inference. We do not sell your personal information. We may disclose information if required by law, to protect rights and safety, or in connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale subject to appropriate safeguards and notice where required.

6. International transfers

Your information may be processed in Japan and other countries. Where required, we implement appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent mechanisms.

7. Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for the purposes described, unless a longer period is required by law. Backup copies may persist for a limited period under technical controls.

8. Security

We implement administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect personal information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

9. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal information, to object to certain processing, and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. Contact us to exercise rights. We will respond within the timeframes required by applicable law.

10. Children

The Services are not directed to children under 16 (or the age required by local law). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Cookies

We may use cookies and similar technologies for functionality, preferences, and analytics. You can control cookies through browser settings.

12. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised policy and revise the "Last updated" date. Material changes will be communicated as required by law.

13. Contact
