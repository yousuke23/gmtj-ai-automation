FAQ

Please confirm pricing, schedules, and participation rules with each program and official contact channel.

Can AI handle bookings or payments?

No. On this site, the AI concierge and our operating model route bookings, payments, and cancellations to human staff (e.g. email). Full automation needs separate systems and legal review.

Should I send personal or passport data?

Please don’t. Avoid entering sensitive data in chat or forms; use official channels for required procedures.

Which regions do you cover?

Music tourism broadly. For region-specific work we prioritize alignment with official tourism bodies, municipalities, and partners—with clear sourcing for answers.

Can we publish other companies’ logos or music?

Rights and credits must be confirmed per case. Set a publishing policy and templates, then share them with the relevant stakeholders.

How long does rollout take?

Pilots often start in weeks; production depends on security readiness and data hygiene. We begin with a scoped discovery.
