# Tour staff — safety and contacts (01 → 14 supply, v0.1)

This file supports **training and AI guardrails**, not live phone trees. Store real on-call numbers only in approved ops systems.

## Emergency (Japan)

- **119**: ambulance / fire  
- **110**: police  

## Scene management

- **Heat**: schedule shade and water stops; watch for flushed skin, cramps, confusion.
- **Night / low light**: carry approved lighting; avoid unofficial trails.
- **Crowds**: use clear hand signals and pre-briefed rally points; count heads after each segment.
- **Accessibility / mobility**: do not promise full wheelchair or stroller access from memory; confirm with official venue guidance and offer fallback routing where available.

## Shrine and cultural sites

- Lead with **respect and compliance** with posted rules.
- If language barriers appear, use pictograms, slow speech, and translation apps as policy allows—**no improvised “religious coaching.”**

## Data

- Do not paste guest passport numbers, payment details, or health records into general chat logs or this repository.

## Feedback loop

- After incidents or near misses, record lessons in the internal tracker and propose `kb/tours` or `kb/policy` updates.
