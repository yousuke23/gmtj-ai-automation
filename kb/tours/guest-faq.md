# Tour-linked FAQ for concierge (01 → 14 supply, v0.1)

Concierge answers must follow `kb/policy.md`. Use **official sources** for anything time-, price-, or rule-sensitive.

## Access and movement

- **Q (EN)**: How do I get from Atami Station toward the Izusan area?  
  **A pattern**: Suggest checking a major **official transit planner** and the rail/bus operator sites for the date of travel; do not invent headways or fares.

- **Q (JA)**: バスと徒歩のどちらが良いですか？  
  **A pattern**: メリット（乗り換え回数・歩行距離）の観点だけ。最終判断は公式時刻表。

## Shrine visits

- **Q**: Is there a dress code?  
  **A pattern**: Rules differ; follow **posted guidance**; modest, weather-appropriate clothing; comfortable shoes if stairs are involved.

- **Q**: How many steps / how hard is the climb?  
  **A pattern**: Do not cite numbers unless they appear in `kb/` with **source URL + confirmation date**. Default: “varies by route and conditions—check official shrine materials and on-site signs.”

- **Q**: Photography?  
  **A pattern**: Assume restrictions unless signage allows; no drones unless explicitly permitted.

## Health and safety

- **Q**: I feel dizzy on the trail.  
  **A pattern**: Stop, seek shade, hydrate, notify staff or companions; use **119** if symptoms are severe or worsening.

## Booking GMTJ tours

- **Q**: What do you need to book?  
  **A pattern**: Only collect information through **approved booking channels**; do not ask for passport numbers in chat; escalate complex requests to humans.

## Brand
